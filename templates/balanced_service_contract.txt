SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Service Agreement is entered into on April 10, 2024

BETWEEN:
GreenLeaf Enterprises, a partnership firm registered in Chennai, Tamil Nadu ("Client")

AND:
Digital Marketing Solutions LLP, having office in Hyderabad, Telangana ("Service Provider")

1. SERVICES
The Service Provider shall provide digital marketing and social media management services as detailed in Annexure A.

2. TERM
This Agreement shall be effective from May 1, 2024 for an initial period of 12 months. Either party may choose to renew by mutual written consent 30 days before expiry.

3. COMPENSATION
3.1 Monthly retainer: Rs. 75,000 (Rupees Seventy Five Thousand only) plus applicable GST
3.2 Payment due within 30 days of invoice
3.3 Late payment interest: 1% per month after 30 day grace period

4. TERMINATION
4.1 Either party may terminate with 60 days written notice
4.2 Termination for cause (material breach) requires 15 days notice and opportunity to cure
4.3 Upon termination, Client shall pay for services rendered until termination date

5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
5.1 Pre-existing IP of each party remains with that party
5.2 New IP created specifically for Client campaigns shall belong to Client
5.3 Service Provider retains right to use generic marketing materials and methodologies
5.4 Service Provider may showcase work in portfolio with Client's consent

6. CONFIDENTIALITY
Both parties shall maintain confidentiality of proprietary information for 3 years post-termination. This excludes publicly available information and information required to be disclosed by law.

7. LIABILITY AND INDEMNIFICATION
7.1 Each party shall indemnify the other for breaches of this Agreement
7.2 Total liability capped at 6 months of fees paid
7.3 Neither party liable for indirect or consequential damages

8. PERFORMANCE STANDARDS
Service Provider shall use commercially reasonable efforts to achieve KPIs outlined in Annexure A. Failure to meet targets for 2 consecutive months allows Client to terminate without penalty.

9. AMENDMENTS
Any amendments must be in writing and signed by both parties. No unilateral changes permitted.

10. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
10.1 Disputes shall first be attempted to be resolved through good faith negotiations
10.2 If unresolved within 30 days, parties may pursue mediation
10.3 Arbitration in Chennai under Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996
10.4 Each party bears own costs unless arbitrator decides otherwise

11. FORCE MAJEURE
Neither party liable for delays due to circumstances beyond reasonable control including natural disasters, strikes, or government actions. Affected party must notify within 7 days.

12. INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR
Service Provider is an independent contractor, not an employee. Responsible for own taxes and compliance.

13. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by Indian laws. Courts in Chennai shall have jurisdiction.

14. ENTIRE AGREEMENT
This Agreement and annexures constitute the complete agreement. Supersedes all prior discussions and understandings.
