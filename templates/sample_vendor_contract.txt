VENDOR SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Agreement is entered into on January 15, 2024

BETWEEN:
ABC Technologies Pvt. Ltd., a company incorporated under the Companies Act, 2013, having its registered office at Mumbai, Maharashtra (hereinafter referred to as "Client")

AND:
XYZ Solutions Private Limited, having its office at Bangalore, Karnataka (hereinafter referred to as "Vendor")

1. SCOPE OF SERVICES
The Vendor agrees to provide software development services to the Client as per the specifications provided by the Client from time to time.

2. PAYMENT TERMS
2.1 The Client shall pay the Vendor a monthly fee of Rs. 5,00,000 (Rupees Five Lakhs only).
2.2 Payment shall be made within 60 days of invoice submission.
2.3 In case of delay beyond 60 days, a penalty of 2% per month shall be applicable on the outstanding amount.

3. TERM AND TERMINATION
3.1 This Agreement shall commence on February 1, 2024 and continue for a period of 24 months.
3.2 This Agreement shall automatically renew for successive periods of 12 months unless either party provides written notice 90 days prior to expiry.
3.3 The Client may terminate this Agreement at its sole discretion with 30 days written notice without providing any reason.
3.4 The Vendor may only terminate with 180 days notice and upon payment of liquidated damages equal to 6 months fees.

4. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
4.1 All intellectual property rights, including but not limited to copyrights, patents, and trade secrets developed during the term of this Agreement shall vest exclusively with the Client.
4.2 The Vendor assigns all present and future IP rights to the Client without any additional compensation.

5. INDEMNIFICATION
5.1 The Vendor shall indemnify and hold harmless the Client from any and all claims, damages, losses, and expenses arising out of the Vendor's performance or non-performance under this Agreement.
5.2 The Vendor's liability under this clause shall be unlimited and shall survive the termination of this Agreement.

6. CONFIDENTIALITY
6.1 The Vendor shall maintain strict confidentiality of all Client information for a period of 5 years after termination.
6.2 Breach of confidentiality shall result in immediate termination and liquidated damages of Rs. 50,00,000.

7. NON-COMPETE
7.1 During the term of this Agreement and for a period of 3 years thereafter, the Vendor shall not engage in any business that competes with the Client's business in India.
7.2 The Vendor shall not solicit or provide services to any client of the Client during this period.

8. FORCE MAJEURE
Acts of God, war, or government regulations shall constitute force majeure events, provided the Client determines such events are beyond reasonable control.

9. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
9.1 Any disputes arising out of this Agreement shall be resolved through arbitration in Mumbai.
9.2 The arbitration shall be conducted in accordance with the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996.

10. AMENDMENTS
The Client may modify or amend any terms of this Agreement at its sole discretion by providing written notice to the Vendor. Such amendments shall become effective immediately upon notice.

11. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of India. The courts of Mumbai shall have exclusive jurisdiction.

12. ENTIRE AGREEMENT
This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior understandings.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement on the date first written above.
