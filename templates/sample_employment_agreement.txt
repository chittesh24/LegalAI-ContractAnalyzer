EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement is made on March 1, 2024

BETWEEN:
TechCorp India Pvt. Ltd., registered office at Pune, Maharashtra (hereinafter called "Employer")

AND:
Mr. Rajesh Kumar, residing at Delhi (hereinafter called "Employee")

TERMS AND CONDITIONS:

1. POSITION AND DUTIES
The Employee is appointed as Senior Software Engineer and shall perform duties as assigned by the Employer from time to time.

2. COMPENSATION
2.1 The Employee shall be paid a monthly salary of Rs. 1,20,000 (Rupees One Lakh Twenty Thousand).
2.2 Annual performance bonus may be provided at the sole discretion of the Employer.

3. PROBATION PERIOD
The Employee shall be on probation for 6 months. The Employer may terminate employment during probation without notice or compensation.

4. WORKING HOURS
The Employee shall work a minimum of 45 hours per week. Additional hours may be required without overtime compensation as this is a managerial position.

5. LEAVE POLICY
The Employee is entitled to 18 days of paid leave per year, subject to Employer approval.

6. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
All work product, inventions, and creative works developed by the Employee during employment shall belong exclusively to the Employer, including work done outside office hours or using personal resources.

7. CONFIDENTIALITY AND NON-DISCLOSURE
7.1 The Employee shall maintain confidentiality of all Employer information perpetually.
7.2 This obligation survives termination of employment indefinitely.

8. NON-COMPETE AND NON-SOLICITATION
8.1 For 2 years after termination, the Employee shall not work for any competitor of the Employer.
8.2 The Employee shall not solicit Employer's clients or employees for 3 years post-termination.
8.3 This restriction applies pan-India and to any related technology sector.

9. TERMINATION
9.1 The Employer may terminate this employment immediately without cause or notice.
9.2 The Employee must provide 3 months notice to resign.
9.3 The Employer may accept resignation immediately without paying notice period salary.

10. POST-TERMINATION OBLIGATIONS
Upon termination, the Employee shall:
- Return all company property immediately
- Delete all company data from personal devices
- Not make any negative statements about the Employer
- Be available for transition support for up to 60 days without compensation

11. PERSONAL GUARANTEE
The Employee personally guarantees that all project deliverables will meet quality standards. Any client losses due to the Employee's work shall be recovered from the Employee.

12. AMENDMENT
The Employer reserves the right to modify employment terms including salary, position, and location with 15 days notice.

13. JURISDICTION
This agreement is governed by Indian laws and courts of Pune shall have exclusive jurisdiction.

ACCEPTANCE:
I have read and understood all terms and accept them unconditionally.
